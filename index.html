How to Play

The Board

9 mini tic-tac-toe boards arranged in a 3×3 grid. Where you place your mark determines which mini-board your opponent must play in next.

Asymmetric Win Conditions

⚡ Harry wins by claiming 3 mini-boards in a row on the meta-grid.

☠ Voldemort wins by corrupting 5 out of 9 mini-boards total (no row needed).

Spells (Once Per Game)

✨ Expelliarmus — Remove one of Voldemort's marks.

🛡 Patronus Shield — Protect a board for 2 turns.

⚡ Avada Kedavra — Claim two adjacent empty cells.

☠ Dark Mark — Corrupt one of Harry's marks.

Casting a spell uses your turn. Your opponent gets free choice of board afterward.

Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe Rule

The cell you play in (0-8) sends your opponent to the corresponding mini-board. If that board is already completed, they can play anywhere.
